Quick Math Trainer

Summary:

  • Quick Math Trainer was created with the Unity Game Engine and C# scripting and is available on Android OS.
  • Practice arithmetic questions against the clock.
  • How To Play:
    1. Choose one or multiple operators: Add, Subtract, Multiply, Divide.
    2. Choose a difficulty setting from: Easy, Medium, Hard.
    3. Answer all of the questions correctly before time runs out!

AutoHotKey Volume Control Script

  • This script allows the user to change their volume, switch their audio device, mute their microphone and mute their audio device all with simple, quick keyboard shortcuts.
  • If the user wants to switch between their headphones output device and speakers output device, they just have to press the "start" key + "numpad enter" key.
